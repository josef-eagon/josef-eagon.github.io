The Nut Tree

A Walnut Tree stood proudly in a field. Over time, a road was laid near the tree where passersby would stop and enjoy the shade. The tree bore many fruit, and the travelers would assault the tree with sticks and stones in order to convince the tree to drop more from the higher branches.

"O wretched me," cried the Tree. "I give them my fruit and shade freely, yet this is how they repay me!"
