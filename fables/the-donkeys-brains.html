The Donkey's Brains

A Lion and a Fox decided to hunt for a meal together when the Lion had an idea. He called over to a Donkey and offered a partnership. The Donkey, eager to form a relationship with the royal, trotted over. Immediately, the Lion pounced on the Donkey. “This will be our supper tonight, so do not touch our meal until later,” the Lion cautioned the Fox as he walked off to nap.

The Fox waited a long while, but thinking the Lion might not return, scooped out the brains of the Donkey for a snack. Eventually, the Lion did return, and upon finding the brains missing began interrogation of the Fox.

“Oh, but your Majesty,” the Fox pleaded. “If the Donkey had any brains they would not have fallen for your trap.”
