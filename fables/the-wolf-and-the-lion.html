The Wolf and the Lion

There was a wolf walking alongside the desert at sunset, and in the corner of his eye, the Wolf caught his shadow stretching far. "Why should I have fear, for when even my shadow stretches wide across the desert sand and all the way out to the horizon?" he boasted to himself, feeling proud and strong. "Perhaps I should be acknowledged as king of the beasts!"

As the wolf postured to make his shadow as large and menacing as possible, a lioness descended on the wolf, and devoured him.
