The Astronomer

An Astronomer used to wander about at night staring at the stars. One time she was meandering near the outskirts of a village, fixated on the twinkling of a certain star, and fell into a stream.

Cursing her fall and while accounting her scrapes and bruises, a passerby shouted at her, "How have you managed to stare so long at what is above, and forget what is happening below?"
