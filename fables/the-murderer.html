The Murderer

In a fit of fiery rage, a Man killed another and was forced to flee from the relatives of the slain. After running for some time, the Man reached the River Nile. A pride of lions spotted the Man, weakened from the chase, and added their numbers to his pursuit.

Frightened, the Man climbed a tree near the river to find a serpent resting high in the branches. Out of shock, the man threw himself from the canopy into the river where a crocodile had been silently waiting, and easily devoured him.
