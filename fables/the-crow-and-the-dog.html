The Crow and the Dog

A Crow, while offering a sacrifice to Athena, invited a Dog for the banquet. The Dog inquired, “Why do you sacrifice in vain, for the goddess has scorned you? Your omens are disregarded by the people.”

The Crow cawed back, "Precisely put, Dog. It for this reason, so I might reconcile her enmity towards me."
