The Fox and the Goat

By some chance, a Fox found herself stuck in a well. A Goat passing by called down to the Fox, “How is the water down there? I am quite thirsty from the heat of the day.”

The Fox decided upon a scheme, and invited the Goat to join for a refreshing drink. The Goat hopped down into the well, but after a moment of rest, wondered how they would escape the place.

“I have just the solution,” explained the Fox. “If you stand up tall, I can hop off your horns.” The Goat did as instructed, but once the Fox leapt out and started away, he called out to the Fox for assistance.

“You should have thought it through before jumping into a well, dear Goat!”
