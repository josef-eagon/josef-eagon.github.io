The Fox and the Ape

The Fox and the Ape The thoughtless creatures and irrational animals gathered to elect a new King. The eager Ape performed a dance to delight and entertain, receiving much acclaim. The Fox, envious of the Ape, announced “I have found a delicious treat and saved it for your coronation,” and escorted the Ape to a hunter’s lure.

The Ape was quickly ensnared whereupon the Fox called out, “How do you intend to rule with such foolishness?”
