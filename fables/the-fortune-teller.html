Title

There once was a Mantis living as a Fortune Teller that made his living by sitting in town selling fortunes and mysteries through cards and elixirs. When one day a Termite traveled to alert the Mantis that his home had been ransacked, the Mantis scurried off to investigate the claim.

A Cockroach, living as a neighbor to the Mantis, shouted at him as he fluttered past, "With all you claim to see tomorrow for others, why couldn't you see this today?"
