The Dog and the Hare

A Dog, out for an early morning stroll, saw a Hare stirring and gave chase, quickly catching the creature.

Wounded during capture, the Hare now bled. Seeing the result, the Dog began to lick the wounds clean. "Be a straightforward beast," cried out the Hare, confused by the attack. "Either bite me as an enemy or embrace me as a friend!"
