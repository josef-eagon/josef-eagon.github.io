The Driftwood

There were some travelers walking along the coast whereupon off in the distance they thought they saw a large ship. Deciding it would be nice to watch the ship pull into harbor, they waited and watched as it approached. Soon the travelers realized it was much too small and must be some sort of smaller vessel.

However as the wind blew it closer to shore, it was obvious to be no more than a floating piece of Driftwood. One of the travelers said to the other, “We waited all this time only to watch a stick wash ashore!”

— — — — —

oho! sometimes we perceive things from a distance to be much more than they are.
