The Two Dogs

A man had two Dogs. One was trained to hunt and went out on long days to bring back food for the family. The other remained at the house to serve as a guard. The hunting Dog felt it unfair that the guard Dog ate all the same meals without having to put in the same effort.

“Don’t blame me,” the guard Dog said. “I am only doing what I was trained to do. Blame our master instead.”
