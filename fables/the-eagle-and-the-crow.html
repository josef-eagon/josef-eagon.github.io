The Eagle and the Crow

A Tortoise requested safe passage from an Eagle to a grazing spot on the other side of a rocky outcrop. The Eagle, having nothing better to do, grasped the Tortoise by the shell and took flight.

A Crow seeing the debacle flew up to meet the Eagle saying, “Eagle my good friend, in your talons is a rare delicacy.”,

The Eagle replied, “Can’t you see the shell is too hard to break, Crow?”

The Crow cawed back, “If you agree to share, I can show you how to bash the shell on the rocks below.” The Eagle agreed and dropped the Tortoise as instructed, breaking the shell against the most jagged rocks. Afterwards, the Eagle and Crow enjoyed a nice meal together.
